Recipe #1

  • Time: 50 min
  • Serves: 5
Ingredients Quantity
for dough
Floar 400 g
Water (warm) 200 ml
Salt 1 spoon
for meat
Mince 500 g
Onion 120 g
Water (cold) 100 ml
Grounded red hot pepper to taste
Salt to taste
Utskho-suneli 1 spoon
  1. Mix flour, warm water and salt to make dough.
  2. Prepare the meat filling with onion, spices and cold water.
  3. Fill the dough circles and shape khinkali.
  4. Boil in salted water for 12â€“15 minutes.
Ingredient Explanation
Flour Main ingredient for the dough.
Water Warm water for dough, cold water for juicy meat.
Mince Meat filling, usually beef or beef + pork.
Onion Adds flavor and juiciness.
Salt Enhances taste.
Red pepper Adds spiciness.
